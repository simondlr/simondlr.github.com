Projects I'm really proud of:

- Creator of Tweekly.fm. One of the first 500 Twitter applications. It allowed users to automatically share their music tastes from last.fm to Twitter. Now, a few years on, with the help of Scott Wilcox, it shares to 450 000+ updates a month from 250k users to Twitter and Facebook. A lot of sites depend on Tweekly.fm's updates to Twitter to get an accurate measure of what people are listening to on social media sites.



- Creator of TwimeMachine. A service that allows Twitter users to easily read old tweets. It has been used by over 150 000 people including famous accounts such as Paulo Coelho, Buddy Media, Adidas, Daniel Zuker, Sega and CNBC.
